METAL BENEATH SKIN
------------------
------------------

The year is 2036.

Killer androids, disguised as humans, have infiltrated our society. They walk amongst us waiting for a chance to strike.

As an elite member of the Robot Harassment Team it is your job to seek out and capture these deadly droids.


How to play:
------------

WASD or arrow keys - Movement
CTRL               - Use item
Mouse              - Select item from the menu


Use your FINGER to poke members of the public. Androids make a distinctive clank.

Use your MEGAPHONE to clear the crowds out of your way.

Use the NET GUN to trap suspected androids!

Use the CUTTING TOOL to free members of the public and retrieve your net.


How to win:
-----------

Locate the android and capture it in your net to win!
